# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Sports fans who already trade crypto. They know how fantasy sports work (lineups, contests, leaderboards) and how Solana works (wallets, tokens, transactions). Their context is active trading — they come to Dexi to research athletes, buy/sell tokens, enter contests, and track their portfolio. Speed matters; they're comfortable with on-chain mechanics but don't want friction.

Secondary: pure crypto traders looking for a new prediction-market surface, and traditional fantasy sports players (DraftKings, FanDuel) exploring crypto-native alternatives.

## Product Purpose

Dexi is a decentralized fantasy sports trading platform on Solana. Users trade athlete tokens on automated bonding curves, enter fantasy contests with USDC prizes, and track their portfolios — all on-chain. Success means feeling the energy of sports fandom combined with the precision of on-chain trading, in one fluid interface.

## Brand Personality

Playful, approachable, fast. The feel is confident but not corporate, energetic but not chaotic. Like a premium sports app crossed with a pro trading tool — fun to use, serious about the money.

## Anti-references

- **Generic DeFi / Solana dapps**: dark bg + green accent + glass cards everywhere + blob gradients + purple secondary. Every Solana project looks the same. Dexi should not.
- **Traditional fantasy sports sites**: DraftKings, FanDuel — busy, ad-heavy, cluttered with badges and information noise. Dexi is clean and intentional.
- **Over-gamified crypto**: excessive confetti, spinning wheels, rocket animations. Energy comes from the product, not decoration.

## Design Principles

1. **Energy through restraint.** The product handles money and sports — both are emotional. Visual restraint (breathing room, deliberate color, clean typography) makes the energy feel earned, not performative.
2. **One surface, one job.** Every page has a primary action. Supporting elements recede. Navigation is persistent; chrome is minimal.
3. **Speed as a feature.** Animations are crisp (sub-200ms transitions), data is live, confirmations are instant. Motion is purposeful, never decorative.
4. **Identity without cliché.** No generic Solana green, no glassmorphism default, no gradient text. The brand is carried by type and rhythm, not by decoration.
5. **Confident density.** Trading surfaces are compact without feeling cramped. Marketing is spacious without feeling empty. Density signals confidence when it's intentional.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard best practices: visible focus states, semantic HTML, keyboard-navigable trading surfaces, reduced-motion alternatives for all animations. No formal WCAG compliance target.
